INTRODUCTION

Prof. Sunil Hazari is a tenured Professor in the Department of Marketing, Richards College of Business, University of West Georgia. He was previously (1997-Aug. 2003) Adjunct Professor in the MBA program at the Robert H. Smith School of Business, University of Maryland, College Park. In the past he has held faculty positions at East Carolina University, and George Mason University. Professor Hazari has over 20 years of teaching experience.

His teaching and research interests are in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Web 2.0 Social Media (including the use of wikis, weblogs, and technology platforms such as TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook
